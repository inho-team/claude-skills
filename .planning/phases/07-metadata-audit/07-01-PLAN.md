         - **P3 (Bulk)**: `coding-experts/` skills.
       - Define standardized `invocation_trigger` templates for `coding-experts` sub-categories (e.g., "Use when [Language] expertise is required for implementation or review").
       - Flag skills missing `recommendedModel` or `ContextMemo` sections.
  </action>
